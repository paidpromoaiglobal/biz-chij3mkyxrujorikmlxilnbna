Our Story

Vintage Violet designers

Vintage Violet Designers is a boutique clothing store and tailoring studio nestled in Ganapathi Nagar, Kelambakkam, Chennai. With a passion for fine craftsmanship and an eye for detail, we specialize in custom-stitched blouses, kurtis, and designer outfits that are tailored to fit your unique measurements and style. Our dedicated team brings your wardrobe vision to life, ensuring every stitch reflects elegance and comfort.

Beyond stitching, Vintage Violet Designers also offers Aari embroidery learning experiences, making us a creative hub for fashion enthusiasts. Rated 4.7 out of 5 by our valued customers, we take pride in delivering timely, high-quality work that keeps our clients coming back. Whether you are looking for a perfectly fitted blouse or want to learn the art of Aari work, our doors are open every day from 10:00 AM to 8:30 PM to serve you.
